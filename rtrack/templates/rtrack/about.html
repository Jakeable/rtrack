About rtrack

Rtrack is an attempt to help reddit moderator teams manage and track issues and users. It allows for creating reports that detail problems or issues within a subreddit, hold conversations about them, and associate users and links to them. It also provides more functionality for tracking users of note, by also providing the ability to leave notes and link to modmail conversations.
